# Example: City Manager Annual Financial Disclosure — Daniel Ahn

This is a worked example for the financial-disclosure-formatter skill. It uses the fictional City of Cedar Ridge, TX. City Manager Daniel Ahn is named in the council-ops agenda example (the June 9, 2026 meeting includes an executive session on Mr. Ahn's annual performance evaluation under § 551.074). This disclosure covers calendar year 2025, filed in compliance with the City of Cedar Ridge Code of Ordinances § 2-86 (Annual Statement of Financial Interest) and Tex. Loc. Gov't Code Ch. 176.

## Input

- **Filer:** Daniel Ahn
- **Title:** City Manager, City of Cedar Ridge, TX
- **Filing entity:** City of Cedar Ridge
- **Filing authority:** Helen Marquez, City Secretary (Cedar Ridge City Hall, 200 Live Oak Drive)
- **Filing type:** Annual Statement of Financial Interest under City Code § 2-86, with Form CIS not currently required (no covered Ch. 176 vendor relationship triggers during the period)
- **Period covered:** Calendar year 2025
- **Filing deadline:** March 15, 2026 (per City Code § 2-86)
- **Spouse:** Sarah Ahn (employed as private-sector employee; no contracts with the City)
- **Sources of income above $2,500:** salary from City of Cedar Ridge; rental income from a duplex in Caddo County
- **Business interests:** 35 percent passive ownership in Ahn Family Properties LLC (the entity that owns the duplex)
- **Real property:** primary residence at 412 Magnolia Court, Cedar Ridge; duplex at 905-907 Pine Street, Talbotsville (outside Cedar Ridge, in Caddo County)
- **Gifts during the period:** registration ($475) and travel to the Texas City Management Association annual conference, paid by the conference scholarship fund; no other reportable gifts
- **Boards served:** Texas City Management Association (volunteer; no compensation); Cedar Ridge Chamber of Commerce ex officio (city's standing role)
- **Litigation involving filer in official capacity:** *Smith v. City of Cedar Ridge* (Cause No. 2026-CV-118, 235th District Court of Caddo County) — Mr. Ahn is named in official capacity only; defense managed by the City Attorney
- **Honorarium considerations:** none during the period

## Notes for the User

- This statement is an annual disclosure under the city's local
  ordinance. It is not a Form CIS under Tex. Loc. Gov't Code Ch.
  176 because no vendor-side covered relationship was triggered
  during the period. If during 2026 a vendor with which the filer
  has a covered relationship comes before the City for a contract,
  the filer must file Form CIS within 7 business days under
  § 176.003.
- The TCMA conference scholarship is disclosed here as a
  transparency item under City Code § 2-86 even though it is not
  a "gift from a vendor" under Ch. 176.
- The Caddo County duplex is outside the city's jurisdiction and
  outside the ETJ; no Cedar Ridge action affecting the duplex's
  value is anticipated.
- The City Secretary should post this disclosure on the City
  Secretary's transparency page consistent with the city's adopted
  posting practice.
- This statement was prepared with AI assistance based on the
  filer's responses and was reviewed and signed by Mr. Ahn before
  submission.
```
