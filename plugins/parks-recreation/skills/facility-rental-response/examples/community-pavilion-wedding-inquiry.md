# Example — Live Oak Park Pavilion Wedding Inquiry

This is a worked example for the `facility-rental-response` skill. Input scenario followed by the drafted reply.

## Input scenario

The Cedar Ridge Parks Department rental coordinator received the following email Monday morning:

> Hello — my fiancé and I are getting married in October 2026 and are looking at the Live Oak Park Pavilion for our reception. We are expecting 120 guests, planning a buffet dinner from a local caterer, and we'd like to serve beer and wine. Reception would run from about 5 to 10 p.m. We live in Cedar Ridge. What are the rates and what's the process? Thanks — Priya Mehta (priya.mehta@example.com, 512-555-0188)

Inputs supplied to the skill:

- Facility: Live Oak Park Pavilion
- Date requested: Saturday, October 17, 2026, 5:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m.
- Headcount: 120 guests
- Event type: Wedding reception with buffet and beer/wine service
- Alcohol: yes (beer and wine only)
- Applicant: Priya Mehta, Cedar Ridge resident, priya.mehta@example.com, 512-555-0188
- Calendar status: open
- Cedar Ridge rate schedule (illustrative): Live Oak Pavilion, weekend evening — resident $750, non-resident $1,150, refundable deposit $500
- Alcohol on city property: beer and wine permitted with TABC permit, licensed bartender required, one off-duty officer per 100 guests with alcohol service
- Insurance minimum: $1M per occurrence, $2M aggregate, additional insured
- Amplified sound cutoff: 10:00 p.m.

## Drafted reply

```
Subject: Live Oak Park Pavilion rental inquiry — October 17, 2026

Dear Ms. Mehta,

Congratulations on your engagement, and thank you for thinking of Live
Oak Park for your reception.

AVAILABILITY
Saturday, October 17, 2026, 5:00 p.m. to 10:00 p.m. at the Live Oak
Park Pavilion is currently open. I can place a 7-day hold on the date
while you assemble the paperwork below.

RATES
Live Oak Park Pavilion, Saturday evening, 5:00 p.m.–10:00 p.m.
Resident rate (verified):              $750
Refundable damage deposit:             $500
Add-on — pavilion lighting package:    $125
Add-on — additional cleaning service:  $200 (recommended for receptions
                                        over 100 guests with food)

REQUIRED PAPERWORK
1. Rental Application — submit at cedarridgetx.gov/parks/rentals
2. Certificate of Insurance — general liability, $1,000,000 per
   occurrence and $2,000,000 aggregate, naming the City of Cedar Ridge
   as additional insured
3. Liability Waiver, signed by applicant
4. Event Plan — site layout, vendor list, timeline (required for
   receptions over 100 guests)
5. Security Plan — required because alcohol will be served (see below)

ALCOHOL SERVICE
Beer and wine service is permitted at Live Oak Park Pavilion under the
following conditions:
- A TABC permit appropriate to your service plan must be obtained by
  you or your caterer. The City of Cedar Ridge does not provide the
  permit; please confirm with the Texas Alcoholic Beverage Commission
  whether your caterer's permit covers your event or whether a separate
  one-day permit is required.
- All alcohol must be served by a TABC-certified bartender. Self-service
  is not permitted.
- Service must end at 9:30 p.m. (30 minutes before event end).
- BYOB is not permitted.
- One off-duty Cedar Ridge peace officer is required for the duration
  of the reception (estimated 5 hours, billed at the city's published
  off-duty rate). I can coordinate the assignment through CRPD once
  your application is submitted.

SETUP AND TAKEDOWN
Your rental includes a 1-hour setup window beginning at 4:00 p.m. and
a 1-hour takedown window ending at 11:00 p.m. The pavilion must be
returned to its starting condition; damage or extended cleaning is
billed against the deposit.

NOISE
Amplified sound must end by 10:00 p.m. under Cedar Ridge Code of
Ordinances Ch. 8 (noise). Acoustic performance and ambient conversation
may continue through the 11:00 p.m. takedown.

CANCELLATION
Full refund of fees and deposit if cancellation is received in writing
at least 30 days before the event. 50% refund of fees (deposit refunded
in full) at 14–29 days out. No refund of fees within 14 days; deposit
refunded if no damage.

ACCESSIBILITY
The City of Cedar Ridge complies with Title II of the Americans with
Disabilities Act, 42 U.S.C. § 12131 et seq. Live Oak Park Pavilion is
wheelchair accessible from the main parking lot. Accessible restrooms
are located adjacent to the pavilion. To request a reasonable
modification or auxiliary aid for your event or your guests, please
let me know at least 7 days before the event.

NEXT STEPS
To convert the 7-day hold into a confirmed reservation, please submit
the signed application, certificate of insurance, and $500 refundable
deposit by Monday, May 18, 2026. The hold will expire at 5:00 p.m. that
day. The balance of $750 is due 30 days before the event.

Happy to walk you through any of this on the phone. Reply here or call
me at 512-555-0150.

Lin Cervantes
Rental Coordinator, Parks and Recreation
City of Cedar Ridge | cedarridgetx.gov/parks

This response is an offer to enter into a rental agreement subject to
a signed application and approval by the City. It is not a confirmed
reservation until the application and deposit are received.

AI-assisted draft, reviewed by David Ngata, Parks Director.
```

## Why this reply works

- The first line confirms availability, which is what the applicant came for. Rates and policies come next.
- Alcohol service is treated specifically — TABC permit responsibility, server requirement, end time, and the off-duty officer requirement are all named.
- The noise ordinance citation is concrete (Cedar Ridge Code of Ordinances Ch. 8). A real reply should cite the actual section.
- The hold has an expiration date and time. A hold without paperwork is not a reservation.
- The ADA paragraph names the accessible route, not just the statute.
- The footer paragraph keeps the city from being committed before paperwork lands.
