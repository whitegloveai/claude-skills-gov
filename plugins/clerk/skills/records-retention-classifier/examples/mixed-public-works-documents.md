# Example: Mixed Public Works Documents — City of Cedar Ridge

This is a worked example for the records-retention-classifier skill. It uses the fictional City of Cedar Ridge, TX, which anchors examples across the clerk and council-ops plugins. The Public Works Director, Patel, has asked the City Secretary's office to clear five records out of the FY2025 file room.

## Input

| # | Document | Originating department | Date | Format |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Daily work order log, Streets Division | Public Works | Jan 1 – Dec 31, 2020 | Paper, three binders |
| 2 | Change Order No. 3 to the Magnolia Avenue resurfacing contract (project completed and closed out FY2023) | Public Works | Signed Aug 14, 2022; project closeout Mar 2023 | Paper original + scanned PDF |
| 3 | Service contract with Rio Grande Striping LLC for pavement marking (annual; expired Sep 30, 2024, no renewal) | Public Works | Term Oct 1, 2023 – Sep 30, 2024 | Paper original + scanned PDF |
| 4 | 2018 city-wide traffic count and signal warrant study | Public Works | June 2018; superseded by 2024 update | Paper bound report + GIS data files |
| 5 | Pump test report for Lift Station 4 booster pump retired and replaced in March 2024 | Utilities (Public Works) | Annual reports 2014–2023 | Paper file folder |

No litigation holds. No federal grant funds traced through any of these records. The City Secretary, Marisol Tovar, will sign the disposition log if disposition is recommended.

## Notes for the User

- The work order log is the only record currently eligible for destruction. Confirm with Public Works that the 2020 entries are reflected in the work order management system; the digital record then becomes the official copy and the paper binders may be destroyed.
- The four retained records should be re-flagged in the records management system with the eligibility dates noted so they surface during the next disposition cycle.
- If any of these records are referenced in a pending TPIA request or anticipated litigation (for example, a tort claim arising from a 2022 paving project), suspend disposition immediately and notify the city attorney.
- This example assumes the Public Works director, Patel, the City Manager Daniel Ahn, and the City Secretary Marisol Tovar are coordinating the FY2025 file room cleanup as a single project under the city's records management policy.
