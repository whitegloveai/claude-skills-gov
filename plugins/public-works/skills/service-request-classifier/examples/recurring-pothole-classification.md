# Example — Recurring Pothole Classification, 700 Block Pecan Street

> A citizen submitted a service request through the Cedar Ridge 311 online portal on May 9, 2026 at 11:42 AM reporting a pothole at the 700 block of Pecan Street. The citizen attached a photo and noted that Pecan Elementary is across the street and that children walk this section to school. The dispatcher pulled prior service history and found two requests at the same block (724 Pecan and 712 Pecan) in the trailing 90 days. The classifier triages the new request as a Priority recurring item, routes it to the Streets Division (Public Works Director Renata Hsu's group), and flags the duplicate pattern as a likely drainage problem warranting an inspection rather than a one-off patch.

break or any other utility cut may have contributed. If the
inspection identifies a drainage or utility-related root cause,
escalate to a project-level evaluation rather than continuing
