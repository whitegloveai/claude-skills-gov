# Example: Public Hearing Notice Package — Case Z-2026-04

This is a worked example for the public-hearing-notice skill. It uses the fictional City of Cedar Ridge, TX, and produces the three-part notice package for the rezoning hearing at 1100 Pecan Street. This pairs with the zoning-narrative-writer example and the staff-report-writer example to form a complete case file.

## Input

- **Case:** Z-2026-04
- **Property:** 1100 Pecan Street, Cedar Ridge, TX 75000; Lot 7, Block 4, Pecan Grove Addition; 0.58 acres
- **Request:** Rezone from R-1 Single-Family Residential to NC-1 Neighborhood Commercial
- **Hearing body:** Cedar Ridge City Council (after P&Z recommendation already on the record)
- **Hearing date and time:** Tuesday, June 9, 2026, 6:30 p.m.
- **Hearing location:** Council Chambers, Cedar Ridge City Hall, 200 Live Oak Drive, Cedar Ridge, TX 75000
- **Statutory basis:** Tex. Loc. Gov't Code § 211.007 (zoning notice)
- **Newspaper:** Cedar Ridge Gazette (newspaper of general circulation)
- **Publication date:** Friday, May 22, 2026 (18 days before the hearing)
- **Mailing date:** Tuesday, May 19, 2026 (21 days before the hearing; satisfies the 10-day minimum)
- **Mailing list:** 27 owners of record within 200 feet of the subject property per the most recently approved Caddo County Appraisal District tax roll
- **Posted-sign:** Cedar Ridge Code of Ordinances § 24-303(c) requires a sign posted on the property at least 14 days before the hearing
- **City Secretary:** Helen Marquez

## Notes for the User

- Keep the publisher's affidavit from the Cedar Ridge Gazette with the case file.
- Keep the United States Postal Service Certificate of Mailing (Form 3877) with the 27-name certified mailing list with the case file.
- Photograph the sign in place on the day of posting and keep the photograph with the case file.
- The Planning and Zoning Commission already issued its recommendation on May 12, 2026; that recommendation is on the record and Council is now the hearing body.
- If a protest petition is filed, time-stamp it and count the threshold against the 200-foot owner list before the hearing begins.
