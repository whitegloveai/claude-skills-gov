## 12-MONTH PROBATIONARY REVIEW (CIVIL SERVICE)

The probationary period runs through May 31, 2027. Under Tex. Loc. Gov't Code § 143.027, the Department Head may discharge or demote a probationary employee without right of appeal, but the City should still document performance and adhere to the City's adopted policies and any applicable civil service rules. Final probationary review must be completed before the end of the 12-month period.

- [ ] Final probationary review by Department Head
- [ ] Retention or non-retention decision documented
- [ ] Civil Service Commission notification
- [ ] TCOLE record update if separation occurs
