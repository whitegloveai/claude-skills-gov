## 4. Infrastructure

**Water:** 16-inch main at site boundary, 65 psi static pressure, 4.5 MGD available capacity at the Cedar Ridge water treatment plant after current commitments. Two hydrants within 500 feet of buildable area.

**Wastewater:** 12-inch gravity main at site boundary. Receiving lift station has 1.8 MGD available capacity. Industrial pretreatment program in place; standard distribution-center discharge expected to fit without pretreatment.

**Electric:** Cedar Ridge Light & Power (municipally owned) serves the site. Substation 0.6 miles from site boundary. Available service: 12.47 kV primary with dual-feed option from a second substation at 1.4 miles. Recent substation upgrade completed Q1 2026.

**Natural gas:** 6-inch main at the parkway, 60 psi, ample capacity for distribution-center use.

**Fiber:** Two providers, dual-route diversity, sub-5ms latency to Austin and Dallas data hubs.

**Transportation:** I-35 interchange 4 miles, no weight-restricted bridges. State highway 290 connector under TxDOT capacity study with completion forecast for 2028.
